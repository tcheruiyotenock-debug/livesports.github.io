At Football Live TV, accessible from kaseolodge.org, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we handle data within the application.

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal technical data to ensure app performance, including:

  • Device model and OS version.
  • Local preferences (favorite teams/leagues).
  • Unique device identifiers (Android Advertising ID) for advertising and analytics.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide high-quality service and monetization, we use:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements.
  • OneSignal: For match alerts and news notifications.
  • Firebase: For crash reporting and analytics.

3. Data Usage

Data is used solely to improve the user experience, provide real-time score updates, and display relevant ads. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
