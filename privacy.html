Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At PitchStream, accessible from kaseolodge.org, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by PitchStream and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

PitchStream is designed to provide sports updates with minimal data collection. We may collect:

  • Device Information: To ensure notifications are delivered correctly to your specific model (e.g., Samsung A13).
  • Favorites: Your selected teams and leagues are stored locally or via secure cloud sync to personalize your experience.

2. Background Data Usage

Our app uses background services to provide real-time goal alerts. This process checks for score updates at regular intervals. No personal browsing data or location data is tracked during this process.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our app.
  • Improve and personalize user experience.
  • Send push notifications for match events (goals, start/end).

4. Third-Party Services

We use reliable sports data APIs to provide scores. These providers do not have access to your personal identity. We may use Firebase for notification delivery, which adheres to Google's strict privacy standards.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.
